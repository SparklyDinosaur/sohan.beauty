What is UC9?

UC9 is Sohan's gay pride group — a tight-knit circle of queer people and allies who show up for each other unconditionally. It's a chosen family built on authenticity, unapologetic self-expression, and an unending supply of gay memes.

We believe that queer joy is radical. That visibility matters. That no one should have to shrink themselves to make anyone else comfortable. UC9 is the space where we get to be exactly, fully, gloriously ourselves.

What UC9 Stands For

Radical Love

We love each other fiercely and without condition. In UC9, love is never something you have to earn.

Full Visibility

No hiding. No toning it down. Every member of UC9 gets to take up exactly as much space as they want.

Chosen Family

UC9 is the people who show up. The group chat that never sleeps. The crew that has your back, always.

Gay Joy

We celebrate. We dance. We laugh loudly. Joy is resistance, and UC9 is relentlessly, defiantly joyful.

Safe Space

UC9 is a place where you don't have to explain yourself. You are already accepted, exactly as you are.

Pride Always

Not just in June. Every day is pride in UC9. Every outfit, every post, every existence is a celebration.
